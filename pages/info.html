How to buy your ticket...

Tickets are sold on the day at the ticket office,sometimes from only an hour before departure. At Colombo Fort Station, specific ticket windows are designated for specific routes..

  • Counter 1 - Anuradhapura, Vavuniya, Talaimannar & Jaffna
  • Counters 2 and 8 - Kandy, Hatton, Nanuoya, Haputale, Elle, Badulla
  • Counter 3 - Batticaloa or Trincomalee
  • Counter 4 - any station, 1st & 2nd class tickets only
 Online Train seat booking is not available in Sri Lanka.
