Ella station

Ella is a small town in the Badulla District of Uva Province, Sri Lanka. It is known for the rich bio-diversity, enriched with numerous varieties of floral and fauna. This beautiful town is situated 1,041 metres above from sea level. Nine Arches Bridge is very famouse among ella visitors and the one who visited ella once will not be hesitated to visit twice.

District: Badulla District

Provice: Uva Provice
